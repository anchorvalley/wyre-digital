WYRE Stablecoin

Stable Value on an Unstoppable Network

1:1 Fiat Backed <$0.01 Per Transaction 1M+ TPS Capable

WYRE is a 1:1 USD-backed stablecoin built on the BSV blockchain, designed for enterprise-grade stability and efficiency. Currently in concept stage—join the waitlist for early access.

How It Works

💰

Mint

Deposit fiat, receive WYRE 1:1. Reserves custodied by regulated institutions.

⚡

Transact

Send anywhere in seconds. Sub-cent fees even at enterprise volume.

🔄

Redeem

Convert WYRE back to fiat anytime. No lock-up, no slippage.

✅

Verify

All reserves auditable on BSV public ledger. Trustless proof of solvency.

Why BSV?

  • 📈 Unlimited Scaling (Teranode) — 1M+ TPS
  • 💸 Sub-Cent Transaction Fees — fractions of a cent regardless of load
  • 🛡️ Enterprise-Grade Stability — protocol is locked, no governance drama
  • ⚖️ Regulatory Compliance by Design — transparent ledger supports KYC/AML natively
  • 🔗 Original Bitcoin Protocol — peer-to-peer electronic cash as designed
