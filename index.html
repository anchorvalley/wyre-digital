How Immutable Records Works

1. Hash

Compute a secure SHA-256 hash of your invoices, contracts, or purchase orders to create a unique digital fingerprint.

2. Anchor

Submit the hash via our straightforward API to permanently anchor it on the BSV blockchain, ensuring tamper-proof storage.

3. Verify

Easily retrieve and validate the anchored hash against the blockchain at any time for audits or compliance.

Integrate today with our API at records.anchorvalley.us.
