How It Works

From Fiat to Future in Four Steps

A simple, transparent lifecycle. Every WYRE token is fully collateralized and auditable on-chain at every stage.

Step 01

Mint

Deposit fiat currency and receive WYRE tokens at a 1:1 ratio. Reserves are custodied by regulated institutions and verified on-chain instantly.

Step 02

Transact

Send WYRE anywhere in the world in seconds. BSV's massive block capacity enables sub-cent fees even at enterprise transaction volumes.

Step 03

Redeem

Convert WYRE back to fiat at any time. No lock-up periods, no slippage. Redemptions settle directly to your bank account.

Step 04

Verify

All reserves are transparently auditable on BSV's public ledger. Anyone can verify the full collateral backing at any time — trustless proof of solvency.

Infrastructure

Why the BSV Blockchain?

Not all blockchains are built for financial infrastructure at scale. BSV was designed from the ground up for enterprise-grade, high-throughput payments — making it the only blockchain suitable for a stablecoin that demands both performance and compliance.

Join the Waitlist

  • Unlimited Scaling (Teranode)

    BSV's Teranode architecture supports 1 million+ transactions per second. WYRE can scale to serve global commerce without performance degradation or fee spikes.

  • Sub-Cent Transaction Fees

    Fees remain fractions of a cent regardless of network load. Micropayments, payroll, and point-of-sale all become economically viable at any transaction size.

  • Enterprise-Grade Stability

    The BSV protocol is locked. No governance drama, no surprise forks. The rules of the network are fixed — giving enterprises the predictability they require for financial systems.

  • Regulatory Compliance by Design

    BSV's transparent, auditable ledger supports KYC/AML frameworks natively. WYRE is built for a regulatory environment, not against it — enabling institutional adoption.

  • Original Bitcoin Protocol

    BSV restores and preserves the original Satoshi protocol. This is Bitcoin as designed — a peer-to-peer electronic cash system with unbounded capacity and immutable data storage.
